Project Background

Project Background

This map is a geolibrary that contains decades of oceanography research from the personal collections of Humboldt State University Professor Dr. James Avery Gast and University of Washington Professor Dr. Tommy Thompson. Dr. Gast was mentored by Dr. Thompson and acquired his oceanographic library when Dr. Thompson retired. In addition to teaching oceanography for over 30 years at HSU, Dr. Gast served as the founding director of the Telonicher Marine Laboratory in Trinidad, California from 1964-1970 as well as acquired, retrofitted, maintained, and skippered the various HSU oceanographic vessels during his tenure. These documents are made available free by Thomas Gast & Associates Environmental Consultants, a consulting firm founded by Dr.Gast’s son and Dr. Thompson’s namesake. Documents are being added as time permits.

User Instructions

User Instructions

Use this geolibrary by selecting a point that represents a location associated with particular documents. This will populate the table on the right of the map with the document data. Alternatively, you can search for a particular topic or author in the searchbar above the table, and documents associated with your search criteria will populate. Next, click on the "Add to Cart" button associated with each document to add it to the Document Cart. You can access the Document Cart by pressing the button on the far right of the top navigation bar. To order the documents, fill out the necessary information form, and submit your request. Your request will be processed and your documents will be sent to your email as time permits.
